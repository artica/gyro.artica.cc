Education

Gyro is the perfect assistant for any teacher. Use this nice robot to teach your students the new skills of the modern world - programming, electronic and robotic - but also to prepare fun lesson plans about any topic, from math to arts.

Search dozen of lesson plans validated by our team according to National Curriculums or open the lesson plans submitted by other teachers. You can also share your lesson plans and ideas with the Gyro community.

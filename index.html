Open & Personal

Your robot, toy and friend

With Gyro you can have fun while learning robotics behaviors and programming. You can also use it to study other subjects, from math to arts.
Gyro is an Edutainment Robot that is prepared with features like draw, play sounds, remote control, and more.

Just download Gyro's free mobile apps and start playing with your new friend immediately.

Discover . Explore . Learn

If you go beyond the pre-defined shell and movile apps, you can program and customize your Gyro into a new world of infinite possibilities.
Gyro is equipped with plenty of electronics goodies to enable your curiosity and creativity.

Open Design

We really mean it, unlike most educational robots Gyro is all hackable, and fully compatible with Arduino shields.

We invite you to open the shell and add your own sensors and actuators without losing the warranty.

Creativity Enabler

With our free visual programming tool Visualino, you can program Gyro to go much beyond the pre-programmed features.

Gyro reacts to gestures, sounds, colors and lights.

Make it avoid obstacles or chase objects are just a few tasks that Gyro can 'learn' with you.
